The Games We Play!

Superhot VR

Superhot VR is an FPS game that was originally only on PC, however has been ported to the Oculus Quest. Superhot's concept is time only moves when you do, when you stand completely still, time freezes.

Dungeons and Dragons

Dungeons and Dragons is a role-playing game based around massive fantasy war battles. The core premise of DnD is storytelling, to make a story with your friends, create quests, collect treasure, etc.

Pandemic

Pandemic is a co-opertive board game where 2-4 players are tasked with stopping 4 diseases that threaten a region. These players must work together to find a cure for all 4 diseases before one of several game-ending conditions are reached.

Munchkin

Munchkin is a card-based game created to mock role-players who used the "powergaming" tactic (overpowering your character to beat everyone). The goal is to reach level 10 before your opponents by either defeating enemies or sabotaging your fellow players.

Guillotine

Guillotine is a card game where players take turns in killing a noble at the beginning of a line, however, players can play cards to manipulate the order of the line. The objective is to get the most kills after three "days" of chopping.

Raw Data

Raw Data is sci-fi PvE FPS game, but specifically for VR. Go it alone or play with a friend, battling through armies of enemies, using futuristic abilities and fighting styles.
